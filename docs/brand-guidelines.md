# Comanifest — visual identity & brand guidelines

**Archetypal / whimsical / energetic**

Living reference for look, feel, and brand direction. Expand and revise as the product evolves.

For product voice and UI vocabulary (manifest / hold / manifestation), see [`comanifest-brief.md`](./comanifest-brief.md). For what's implemented in code today, see [`project-handoff.md`](./project-handoff.md).

---

## 1. Brand essence

Comanifest is a digital gathering place where people pool intention, imagination, and subtle energy to influence outcomes in the world. It sits at the intersection of:

- collective imagination
- symbolic action
- Jungian synchronicity
- playful magic
- communal ritual
- light-touch spirituality

The brand should feel like a portal — not to another world, but to the deeper layers of this one.

**Emotional tone:** “A gentle shimmer of possibility.”

---

## 2. Brand personality

Comanifest is:

- whimsical but grounded
- mystical but not esoteric
- communal but not earnest
- playful but not childish
- archetypal but not heavy
- magical but not woo-woo

**Think:** Carl Jung meets Studio Ghibli meets a friendly cosmic librarian.

---

## 3. Visual style overview

The visual identity should evoke:

- interconnectedness (threads, constellations, webs, ripples)
- subtle energy (glows, gradients, soft pulses)
- archetypes (circles, mandalas, symbols)
- collective imagination (floating shapes, shared motifs)
- gentle magic (sparkles, light trails, soft motion)

The UI should feel like a quiet ritual space — warm, inviting, slightly enchanted.

---

## 4. Colour palette (energetic + archetypal)

### Primary colours

| Name | Hex | Meaning |
|------|-----|---------|
| **Deep Midnight Indigo** | `#1A1B2E` | The unconscious, the void, the dream-field |
| **Soft Cosmic Lavender** | `#C7B7FF` | Intuition, imagination, subtle energy |
| **Warm Dawn Peach** | `#FFDAC1` | Human warmth, connection, shared presence |
| **Moonlit Silver** | `#E5E7EB` | Reflection, clarity, gentle illumination |

### Accent colours

Use sparingly, like sparks of intention:

| Name | Hex | Meaning |
|------|-----|---------|
| **Electric Teal** | `#2EE6D6` | Synchronicity, flow, alignment |
| **Golden Ember** | `#FACC15` | Focus, intention, collective fire |
| **Rose Quartz** | `#F9A8D4` | Compassion, softness, emotional resonance |

The palette should feel dreamlike but not saccharine, cosmic but not sci-fi.

---

## 5. Typography (archetypal + human)

### Primary typeface

**Inter** or **Satoshi** for clarity and modernity.

### Secondary / display typeface

A subtle, slightly mystical serif for headings, e.g.:

- Cormorant Garamond
- EB Garamond
- Spectral

This creates a ritual-meets-modern contrast.

### Typographic tone

- flowing
- airy
- generous spacing
- soft curves
- no shouting
- no rigid geometry

Headings should feel like chapter titles in a book of symbols.

---

## 6. Layout & spacing (ritual space)

### Principles

- lots of negative space
- soft edges
- circular motifs
- gentle layering
- floating elements
- asymmetry that feels organic

### Spatial metaphor

The interface should feel like:

- a circle of people
- a constellation map
- a shared altar
- a field of intention

Use spacing to create a sense of breath and invitation.

## 7. Logo mark

**Concept:** string-art constellation — seventeen nail-stars, cosmic threads woven in the outer ring (clear centre for the manifestation). **Midnight medallion** favicon on `#1A1B2E`.

| Element | Meaning |
|---------|---------|
| Central ember | Shared focus, collective intention |
| Stars (uneven ring) | People, each holding their own manifestation |
| Constellation threads | Connection, interdependence, synchronicity |

**Style:** organic hand-drawn linework, thin connections, soft cosmic gradients, gentle glow, dreamlike but minimal. Archetypal and Jungian; magical, **not** religious.

**Palette (mark):** midnight `#1A1B2E` + moonlit silver / white — night-sky constellation; light- and dark-background SVG variants.

**Canonical prompt & assets:** [`.design/logo-prompt.md`](../.design/logo-prompt.md)  
**Files:** `public/brand/comanifest-logo-mark.svg` (primary), `.png` (raster concept), `app/icon.svg` (favicon)

Pair the mark with the wordmark **Comanifest** — do not embed text in the icon.

---

## 8. Iconography & symbolic language

### Shape language

- **circles** — wholeness, cycles
- **spirals** — growth, unfolding
- **constellations** — connection
- **threads** — interdependence
- **ripples** — impact

### Icon style

- thin linework
- soft curves
- slightly imperfect, hand-drawn feel
- subtle glow or gradient accents

Avoid corporate icon sets entirely.

---

## 9. Motion & interaction (energetic subtlety)

### Motion tone

- slow
- organic
- easing curves
- gentle pulses
- soft fades
- micro-glimmers

### Examples

- a manifestation card that glows faintly when hovered
- a ripple animation when someone joins a manifestation
- a constellation line that draws itself when a new intention is added

Motion should feel like energy moving, not UI reacting.

---

## 10. Imagery & illustration (collective unconscious)

### Imagery style

- abstract cosmic shapes
- dreamlike gradients
- symbolic motifs
- floating orbs
- constellations
- mandala-like patterns
- soft light textures

### Avoid

- literal “manifestation” imagery
- stock photos
- spiritual clichés (chakras, lotus flowers, etc.)
- anything doctrinal or religious

The imagery should feel like archetypes emerging from mist.

---

## 11. Brand voice (mythic + playful)

For UI copy rules and the manifest / hold vocabulary, see [`comanifest-brief.md` → Voice & vocabulary](./comanifest-brief.md#voice--vocabulary). Shared strings live in **`lib/manifestations/intention-copy.ts`**.

### Tone

- warm
- lightly mystical
- poetic
- curious
- communal
- non-directive

### Language style

- metaphor
- archetype
- gentle invitation
- symbolic resonance

### Examples

- “Add your spark to this intention.”
- “This manifestation is gathering momentum.”
- “What outcome would you like to call into the world?”
- “A constellation forms when many points of light align.”

### Avoid

- self-help jargon
- spiritual dogma
- corporate tone
- productivity framing

---

## 12. What Comanifest is not

- not a petition site
- not a political tool
- not a spiritual doctrine
- not a coaching platform
- not a wellness app
- not a productivity system

It is a playful, communal experiment in shared imagination.

---

Comanifest’s visual identity should feel like:

**“A softly glowing constellation of human intention — whimsical, archetypal, communal, and lightly magical.”**

- Jungian collective unconscious
- symbolic resonance
- subtle energy
- shared imagination
- gentle enchantment

This is not neutral.  
This is not corporate.  
This is alive.
